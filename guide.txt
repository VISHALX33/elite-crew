=========================================
      ELITE CREW - PROJECT GUIDE
=========================================

Welcome to Elite Crew! This guide explains how the platform functions for our three primary roles: User, Vendor, and Admin.

-----------------------------------------
1. USER (Patient / Customer)
-----------------------------------------
The User is the primary consumer of the platform.

Key Features:
- Registration & Login: Users can sign up and verify their identity via Email/Phone OTP.
- Browse Services: Search for healthcare services by category (e.g., Doctors, Labs).
- Book Services: Users can book appointments. Price calculation includes 10% TDS and 18% GST.
- Shop: Purchase healthcare products and medicines.
- Wallet Management: 
    - Recharge wallet for easy payments.
    - View transaction history.
    - Receive automatic refunds upon booking cancellations.
- Order/Booking Tracking: Monitor the status of service bookings and product purchases.
- Reviews: Rate and review services after usage.

-----------------------------------------
2. VENDOR (Service Provider / Seller)
-----------------------------------------
Vendors are professionals (like Doctors) or sellers who offer services and products.

Key Features:
- Profile Setup: Register with company details and business address. Accounts require Admin approval.
- Asset Management: 
    - Create and list services or products.
    - Set pricing and descriptions.
    - Toggle availability for bookings.
- Order Management: 
    - View all bookings/purchases made for their items.
    - Update the status of bookings (e.g., Pending -> Confirmed -> Completed).
- Dashboard: Track earnings and service engagement.

-----------------------------------------
3. ADMIN (Platform Moderator)
-----------------------------------------
The Admin has overarching control over the entire ecosystem.

Key Features:
- User Management: Approve or reject Vendor applications. Manage all user accounts.
- Category Management: Create and organize categories for both Services and Products.
- Global Overlook: 
    - View and manage all bookings and transactions across the platform.
    - Monitor platform-wide financial statistics.
- Content Management: Create and update Blogs, Videos, and handle Contact Messages from the "Contact Us" page.
- System Control: Full authority to create, update, or delete any service, product, or category.
